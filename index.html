What it does

Everything you need to capture knowledge

⚡

One-click Capture

Highlight any text or right-click any element. Araloom grabs it with source URL in under a second , no copy-paste chaos.

📋

Structured Forms

Templates turn raw clips into organized fields, like a smart sheet, but built around how you actually browse.

🔗

@mention Linking

Connect related entries. Link a job application to your resume, or a paper to your notes. Context stays attached.

📝

Notes + Highlights

Add personal notes to any capture. Highlight key passages. Your context stays with the source forever.

🎨

Community Templates

Download JSON templates made by others. Import instantly. No setup, just pick, download, start capturing.

📤

Your Data, Always

Export Looms as JSON or CSV. Import templates in one click. No lock-in, no subscriptions, no accounts required.
